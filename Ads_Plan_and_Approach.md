# Ads Plan & Approach (AppLovin MAX)

## Overview
Silk Shooter will use a **rewarded ads only** monetization model. There will be no forced interstitial ads.

## Core Philosophy
- Player choice first
- Rewards should feel valuable but optional
- Keep the tone light and fun

## Chosen Network: AppLovin MAX
- Good Godot support
- Strong for indie rewarded ads

## Planned Placements
1. Pre-Run Bonus Button (Main)
2. Post-Death Continue Option
3. Support Developer Button

## Summary
Optional rewarded ads only. Player-driven. No forced ads.
